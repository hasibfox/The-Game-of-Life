The Travellers between my Doors

I was about 9 years old when I abruptyly woke up, or so I thought I did atleast. It was the same old room: the same moonlight gleamed through the window, the same monster of stacked clothes on my chair glared ferosciously at me, the same fluorescent blue desktop button flickered on and off. Yet, my consciousness trailed elsewhere, towards the corner of the room. A tall, vantawhite figure with no noticable distinctions peaked silently through the crack of the door. I couldn't define an eye, a nose, an ear, or any face at that matter, but I could feel its stare and the eerieness that came with it. I didn't feel the urge to whimper nor wail. As odd as this figure appeared, I felt no hint of hostility coming from it, but rather curiosity. Time passed but the room remained dead silent. I don't know how long it lasted, but eventually, the figure entered the room. His body slouched lethargically and as he inched closer, a family of similarly white figures followed behind. They moved towards the center of room but their heads always faced towards me, almost like an owl. As they finally reached my closet on the opposite side of the room, they stood for one last moment, staring more heavily than before. I couldn't understand them nor there intention buts as they opened my closet and left through this second portal, I had a faint flashback to a memory that was not mine. A bright, lively scene of a family, throwing grass in the air and laughing softly as the air brushed by. Without me realizing, I snapped back to reality, with everything still the same except one: my door and closet slightly opened. I never managed to understand what they were or what that memory meant or whether this experience was real, but since then, I haven't been able to forget them.
